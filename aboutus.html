About The Project

This project aims to help performance students easily calculate the most important altimeter settings. Developed by a group of 2022 students, the website will surely be considered one of the most important resources when it comes to studying for tests. Need any help with the inputs asked? Qfe and qnh correspond to the atmospheric pressure values, respectively at runway level, and sea level. On the other hand, pressure and correction altitude refer to the altitude corresponding to a certain pressure considering ISA conditions and the difference between true altitude and pressure altitude.
